## Data Analysis & GroupBy — Churn Insights (Day 3)

**1. Overall Churn**

- Churn = **26.6%** → concerning

**2. Demographics**

- Gender: no effect
- Senior Citizens: **41.7% churn** (~2× non-seniors)
- Dependents: 15.5% churn (½ of non-dependents)
    - non-dependents: **31.2%**

**3. Services & Subscriptions**

- Fiber optic: **41.9% churn** (highest service risk)
- No TechSupport: **31.2%** (~2× of that with support)
- No OnlineSecurity: **31.4%** (~2× of that with security)
- No DeviceProtection / Streaming: ~5–6% higher churn (relative)

**4. Contract / Payment / Internet Type**

- Month-to-month: **42.7% churn** (highest overall; 15× two-year contracts; 4× one-year contracts)
- Electronic check: **45.3% churn** (highest payment risk)
- Fiber optic: 41.9% (highest)

**5. Tenure Segments**

- 0–12 months: **47.7% churn** (new customers = highest risk)
- after 24 months: churn declines
- 61–72 months: 6.6% churn (most loyal)

**6. Revenue Metrics (ARPU / LTV)**

- Churners pay **21% higher ARPU** → high revenue but low loyalty
- Long-tenure customers generate **+67% LTV**
- Month-to-month customers = highest ARPU (+₹5.5/month) **and** highest churn (42.7%) → high-impact retention target
